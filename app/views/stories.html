What is TNR?

TNR is Trap.Neuter.Return. TNR is one of the most effective ways to handle the feral cat population. Cats establish colonies on their own and these colonies can grow quickly and become disruptive and difficult which is where TNR comes in. We humanely trap, neuter as well as vaccinate, and then re-lease the cat into it's existing colony. Removing a colony is ineffective and the cats often return. TNR provides a way to eliminate nuiscance behavior and procreation, protecting both the cats and community!
